🌍 We Are All Connected

Every human being shares 99.9% of the same DNA. Yet we find millions of ways to divide ourselves — by religion, race, language, nationality, politics, sports teams, income, education, generation, gender, sexuality, ideology, profession, culture, region, and countless other factors.

This visualization shows just how many ways we separate each other. The more factors you see, the more you realize: underneath all these labels, we are all the same.

💫 We Are More Similar Than Different

Every colored bubble represents something that can divide us. But look at what we all share: the desire to love, to be loved, to be safe, to belong, to matter.

The goal isn't to eliminate our differences — it's to recognize that beneath all the labels, we are one human family.
